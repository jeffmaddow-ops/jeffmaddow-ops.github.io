How I Work

I start by assuming the plan is missing something important and my job is to find it before production does.

Every project moves through the same basic pattern: get clear on what "good" looks like for the business, design an integration that can actually survive real traffic, then prove it under conditions that look like day 2, not just day 0.

  • Business alignment — Turn loose goals and RFP language into concrete scope, timelines, and visible risks so nobody is surprised at go-live.
  • Technical implementation — Design and validate integrations end to end: auth, data flow, failure paths — so they're deployable, observable, and supportable.
  • Production stability — Look for how it will fail, not just how it works in the happy path, and close those gaps before they turn into incidents.
